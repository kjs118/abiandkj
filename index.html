About Us

Abi & KJ are an acoustic duo covering a wide variety of songs. You can expect timeless classics, one hit wonders that you forgot you loved, and deep cuts for the music buffs.

Catch Abi & KJ performing their dynamic duo sets around Lafayette and the surrounding areas. Together, they also rock the stage as core members of the hard-hitting classic rock band Thunder Child. Abi stays busy with plenty of solo gigs plus performances with her other projects, including the nostalgic 90s-vibe band OG Cola and the psychedelic indie-rock group Sunkissed.
